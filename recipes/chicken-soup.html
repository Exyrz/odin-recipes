Chicken pot pie soup

Description

Cozy Chicken Pot Pie Soup - All the comforting flavors of a classic chicken pot pie, but in a creamy, spoonable soup! Tender chicken, hearty vegetables, and a velvety broth come together with a hint of herbs and a flaky pastry topping (or buttery croutons) for the ultimate bowl of warmth. Perfect for chilly days or whenever you crave homestyle comfort in every sip!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil, divided
  • 4 to 6 bone-in, skin on chicken thighs
  • 1 teaspoon all-purpose seasoning, plus more as needed
  • 1 large yellow onion, chopped
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 4 carrots, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 cup mushrooms, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cube chicken bouillon, or more to taste
  • 2 teaspoons dried rosemary
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 (12 ounce) bag mixed frozen vegetables
  • 4 cups chicken stock, or more as needed
  • 1 cup heavy cream or half-and-half
