Chicken pot pie soup

Description

Cozy Chicken Pot Pie Soup - All the comforting flavors of a classic chicken pot pie, but in a creamy, spoonable soup! Tender chicken, hearty vegetables, and a velvety broth come together with a hint of herbs and a flaky pastry topping (or buttery croutons) for the ultimate bowl of warmth. Perfect for chilly days or whenever you crave homestyle comfort in every sip!
